Our Services

From initial diagnosis to long-term management, we offer a complete spectrum of sleep apnea care under one roof.

What We Provide

Eight Specialized Services

Each service is designed to address a specific aspect of sleep apnea diagnosis, treatment, and management.

🔬

Sleep Apnea Diagnosis

Comprehensive evaluation using validated questionnaires, physical examination, and the latest diagnostic criteria. We leave no stone unturned in determining the type, cause, and severity of your sleep-disordered breathing.

Learn More
💨

CPAP Therapy

We provide expert CPAP fitting, pressure titration, mask selection, and long-term compliance support. Our CPAP specialists help you choose the right device and ensure maximum comfort so therapy actually works.

Learn More
😁

Oral Appliance Therapy

Custom-fabricated mandibular advancement devices (MADs) that gently reposition the lower jaw during sleep. An excellent alternative for patients with mild to moderate OSA or those who cannot tolerate CPAP.

Learn More
🛌

In-Lab Sleep Studies

Full overnight polysomnography in our state-of-the-art sleep lab. Monitors brain activity (EEG), eye movements, muscle activity, heart rate, oxygen levels, and breathing to provide the most detailed diagnostic picture available.

Learn More
🏠

Home Sleep Testing

Convenient, accurate sleep apnea testing in the comfort of your own bedroom. Our validated home sleep test devices record the key data your physician needs, and results are typically available within 48–72 hours of device return.

Learn More
🥗

Lifestyle Counseling

Evidence-based guidance on weight management, dietary changes, positional therapy, exercise, and alcohol and tobacco cessation. Lifestyle modifications can significantly reduce apnea severity and improve treatment outcomes.

Learn More
🏥

Surgical Options

For select patients, surgical interventions may provide lasting relief. We work with top ENT surgeons and oral maxillofacial specialists to evaluate candidacy for procedures including UPPP, MMA, hypoglossal nerve stimulation, and more.

Learn More
👶

Pediatric Sleep Apnea

Specialized evaluation and age-appropriate treatment for children ages 2–18. Our pediatric sleep specialists collaborate with ENT surgeons, dentists, and behavioral therapists to address every aspect of your child's sleep health.

Learn More
How It Works

Your Path to Better Sleep

Our streamlined process gets you from concern to diagnosis to treatment as efficiently as possible.

1️⃣

Initial Consultation

Meet with a sleep specialist to discuss your symptoms, medical history, and sleep concerns. We determine the best diagnostic path for you.

2️⃣

Sleep Study

Complete an in-lab or home sleep test. Our technology captures all the data needed to make an accurate, comprehensive diagnosis.

3️⃣

Personalized Treatment

Receive a tailored treatment plan developed specifically for your diagnosis, lifestyle, anatomy, and personal preferences.

4️⃣

Ongoing Support

Regular follow-up appointments, compliance monitoring, and equipment support ensure your treatment continues to work long-term.
