Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

🎯 Our Mission

To provide the highest standard of sleep medicine care by combining advanced diagnostic technology with personalized treatment plans, empowering every patient to achieve restorative sleep and optimal health. We are committed to making quality sleep care accessible, compassionate, and evidence-based for all who need it.

"Every person deserves the life-changing benefits of a good night's sleep. We exist to make that possible."

🔭 Our Vision

To be the leading center of excellence in sleep medicine — a place where patients receive diagnosis and treatment within the shortest possible time, supported by a multidisciplinary team of sleep specialists, pulmonologists, cardiologists, and dentists working in seamless collaboration.

We envision a world where sleep apnea is no longer the silent epidemic — where awareness, early detection, and accessible treatment transform millions of lives each year.

Our Journey

Our Story

Founded in 2008 by Dr. Sarah Mitchell and Dr. James Patel, the Sleep Apnea Center began as a small, two-physician practice with a single sleep lab and a mission: to bring world-class sleep medicine to patients who had been told their fatigue was "just stress" for years.

Over the next decade, we grew into one of the region's most trusted sleep medicine practices, expanding our team to include pediatric specialists, dental sleep medicine experts, and behavioral sleep therapists. In 2015, we opened our state-of-the-art home sleep testing program, making diagnosis more convenient and affordable than ever.

Today, our center serves thousands of patients annually across eight comprehensive services, with outcomes that consistently exceed national benchmarks. We have trained more than 100 primary care physicians in sleep apnea recognition and referral, and our research has been published in leading sleep medicine journals.

But what we're most proud of is simpler than any award: the thousands of patients who now wake up each morning feeling truly rested, energized, and healthy.
